## Executive Summary

### Platform Overview

An **enterprise-grade, white-label AI guidance platform** delivering real-time, multi-modal assistance through two deployment modes:

1. **Embedded Widget**: Cost-optimized SSE-based chat with optional screen sharing
2. **Standalone Meeting Platform**: Full-featured LiveKit-powered sessions

### Core Innovation

**80% cost reduction** through intelligent architecture:
- SSE (Server-Sent Events) for text chat → 95% cheaper than WebRTC
- LiveKit WebRTC only when screen sharing is needed
- Smart provider routing: Gemini Flash 2.5 (85%) + Claude 3.5 Sonnet (15%)
- 1 FPS screen capture optimization

### Market Opportunity

- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: $15.2B digital adoption platform market
- **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: $4.8B AI-powered guidance segment
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: $240M (5% of SAM in 3 years)

### Competitive Advantage

- **10x cheaper** than WalkMe/Whatfix ($99/month vs $1,000+/month)
- **Embeddable** unlike Microsoft Copilot Vision
- **Screen understanding** unlike traditional chatbots
- **White-label** enabling partner revenue sharing
- **Multi-modal** (voice, vision, text) for accessibility
