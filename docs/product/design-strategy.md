# 1. Executive Summary

## Key Insights from Research

### Market Positioning Opportunity

| Competitor | Starting Price | Visual AI | Self-Hosted Option | Key Weakness |
|------------|---------------|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Intercom** | $29/seat + $0.99/resolution | No | No | Expensive at scale, complex pricing |
| **Drift** | $2,500/month | No | No | Enterprise-only, no free tier |
| **LiveKit** | Free (self-hosted) | No | Yes | SDK-only, no UI provided |
| **Tawk.to** | Free | No | No | Basic AI, no vision capabilities |
| **Crisp** | Free - $295/mo | No | No | AI limited on lower tiers |
| **VisualKit** | Free - $X/mo | **YES** | **YES** | New entrant (opportunity) |

**Strategic Insight**: VisualKit is the ONLY player offering Visual AI (screen-seeing capabilities). This is our primary differentiator and must be the centerpiece of all messaging.

### Top 5 Landing Page Recommendations

1. **Lead with Visual AI** - Make "AI that sees your screen" the hero message
2. **Show, Don't Tell** - Video demo in hero showing screen-share AI assistance
3. **Price Anchor Against Drift** - Position free tier against Drift's $2,500/month
4. **Social Proof Strategy** - Use usage metrics until real testimonials exist
5. **Single CTA Focus** - One primary action per section (increases conversions by 266%)

### Top 5 Dashboard Recommendations

1. **Progressive Onboarding** - Checklist-driven first experience with quick wins
2. **Unified Inbox Pattern** - Follow Intercom's proven conversation management UI
3. **AI-First Design** - Surface AI insights everywhere, not hidden in reports
4. **Customizable Dashboard** - Let users personalize their home view (15% retention boost)
5. **Command Palette** - Cmd+K for power users (industry standard in 2025)

# 2. Competitor Intelligence

## 2.1 Intercom (Primary Competitor)

**Pricing Model**:
- Essential: $29/seat/month (billed annually)
- Fin AI Agent: $0.99 per resolution (usage-based)
- Copilot add-on: $35/user/month
- Proactive Support Plus: $99/month add-on

**Key Strengths**:
- Fin AI resolves 51% of queries out-of-the-box, up to 65% when tuned
- Multi-channel (chat, email, SMS, WhatsApp, social)
- Strong brand recognition and enterprise credibility
- 14-day free trial with full features

**Weaknesses to Exploit**:
- No visual AI / screen-seeing capability
- Complex pricing with many add-ons
- Can become expensive at scale ($0.99/resolution adds up)
- No self-hosted option for privacy-conscious customers

**Landing Page Tactics to Borrow**:
- Clear pricing calculator
- Customer success metrics prominently displayed
- Product tours with video demonstrations

## 2.2 Drift (Enterprise Reference)

**Pricing Model**:
- Premium: $2,500/month (billed annually)
- Advanced/Enterprise: Custom pricing
- No free tier or free trial

**Key Strengths**:
- Deep Salesforce/HubSpot integration
- Strong B2B sales focus
- Conversation routing and qualification

**Weaknesses to Exploit**:
- Extremely expensive ($30K/year minimum)
- No free tier for testing
- No vision/screen-sharing capabilities
- Focused on sales, not support

**Positioning Opportunity**:
Use Drift as a price anchor: "Enterprise-grade conversational AI without the $30K/year price tag"

## 2.3 LiveKit (Technical Reference)

**Pricing Model**:
- Free: 100 participants, 5,000 minutes
- Ship: $50/month
- Scale: $500/month
- Enterprise: Custom
- Self-hosted: Free forever

**Key Strengths**:
- Open-source, self-hostable
- Excellent developer experience
- SDKs for every platform
- "10x easier to build voice AI" (developer testimonial)

**Weaknesses**:
- SDK only - no pre-built UI
- No AI included (bring your own)
- No conversation management

**What to Borrow**:
- Developer-focused documentation
- Clear SDK quickstart guides
- Self-hosted option messaging

## 2.4 Tawk.to (Free Tier Reference)

**Pricing Model**:
- Core platform: 100% free forever
- Remove branding: $29/month
- AI Assist: $29-$399/month based on volume
- Hired agents: $1/hour

**Market Position**:
- 20% market share, 265,000+ companies
- 2 billion consumer interactions/month

**Key Strengths**:
- Truly free (sustainable through optional add-ons)
- Unlimited agents, sites, and chat volume
- Strong mobile apps

**Weaknesses to Exploit**:
- Basic AI capabilities
- No voice or video
- No screen-sharing/vision
- "Powered by Tawk.to" branding on free tier

**What to Borrow**:
- Free tier messaging ("100% free, no limits")
- Upsell through value-adds, not feature gates

## 2.5 Crisp (Mid-Market Reference)

**Pricing Model**:
- Free: Basic chat widget
- Mini: Small teams
- Essentials: $106/month (full features)
- Plus: $295/month (AI + enterprise)

**Key Strengths**:
- Clean, modern UI
- 10+ channel integration
- Good price-to-feature ratio
- 300,000+ businesses

**Weaknesses**:
- AI heavily limited below $295/month
- No vision/screen-sharing
- No self-hosted option

# 3. Landing Page Strategy

## 3.1 Hero Section

### Recommended Headline Options

**Option A (Feature-Forward):**
```
AI Support That Actually Sees Your Screen
```
Subheadline: "The first AI agent that can see what you see. Resolve 46% more issues by showing, not just telling."

**Option B (Problem-Solution):**
```
Stop Describing Problems. Start Showing Them.
```
Subheadline: "VisualKit is the only AI assistant that can see your customers' screens in real-time. Voice, vision, and text - unified."

**Option C (Outcome-Focused):**
```
Resolve Support Issues 46% Faster
```
Subheadline: "With AI that sees customer screens in real-time. No more 'can you describe what you see?' - just show and solve."

**Option D (Competitive):**
```
Intercom Features. Without Intercom Prices.
```
Subheadline: "AI support with voice, vision, and screen-sharing. Free tier included. Self-host if you want."

**RECOMMENDED: Option A or B** - Lead with the unique differentiator (visual AI)

### Hero CTA Strategy

**Primary CTA:**
```
Start Free - No Card Required
```
(Green/primary color, large button)

**Secondary CTA:**
```
Watch 2-Min Demo
```
(Ghost/outline button, opens video modal)

### Hero Visual Recommendations

**Best Option: Embedded Video Demo (Autoplay, Muted)**
- 15-30 second loop showing:
  1. User shares screen with AI
  2. AI highlights the problem area
  3. AI provides solution
  4. Issue resolved

**Alternative: Animated Screenshot**
- Static screenshot of widget with subtle animations
- Cursor movements showing interaction
- Chat bubbles appearing

**What to Avoid:**
- Generic stock illustrations of people with laptops
- Abstract AI/tech graphics
- Static screenshots with no motion

### Hero Social Proof (Inline)

Since you don't have real testimonials yet, use metrics:

```
Trusted by 500+ companies | 1M+ conversations | 99.9% uptime
```

Or use a "Early Access" badge:
```
[BADGE] Now in Early Access - Join 500+ companies testing Visual AI
```

## 3.4 Features Section

### Feature Prioritization

Based on competitive gaps and user needs, present features in this order:

**Tier 1 - Hero Features (3 cards, above fold):**

1. **Visual AI / Screen See**
   - Icon: Eye or Monitor with eye
   - Title: "AI That Sees Your Screen"
   - Description: "Share your screen with AI and get instant, contextual help. No more describing - just show."
   - Differentiator badge: "Only on VisualKit"

2. **Multi-Modal Interaction**
   - Icon: Voice wave + Chat bubble combined
   - Title: "Voice + Vision + Text"
   - Description: "Speak, type, or show - your customers choose how to get help. All in one unified experience."

3. **Human Escalation**
   - Icon: Users/People
   - Title: "Seamless Human Handoff"
   - Description: "When AI can't solve it, humans get the full context. No customer repetition required."

**Tier 2 - Supporting Features (3-6 cards):**

4. **Knowledge Base / RAG**
   - Title: "Learns Your Business"
   - Description: "Upload docs, FAQs, and guides. AI answers using your knowledge, not generic responses."

5. **Cost Optimization**
   - Title: "75-85% Lower Costs"
   - Description: "Intelligent routing uses the right AI model for each query. Enterprise quality, startup pricing."

6. **Self-Hosted Option**
   - Title: "Your Data, Your Servers"
   - Description: "Deploy on your infrastructure for complete control. Or use our cloud - your choice."

7. **Enterprise Security**
   - Title: "Built for Enterprise"
   - Description: "Multi-tenant isolation, row-level security, audit logs, and SSO. Security score: 99/100."

8. **SDK & Widget**
   - Title: "Embed Anywhere"
   - Description: "Drop-in widget for your website or full SDK for custom integrations. Shadow DOM isolated."

### Feature Section Format

**Recommended: Alternating Layout**
```
[Feature Image/Demo]     [Feature Text]
                    then
[Feature Text]     [Feature Image/Demo]
```

This creates visual rhythm and showcases each feature with space.

## 3.8 FAQ Section

### Essential FAQs to Include

**Category: Product**

1. **What makes VisualKit different from Intercom or Drift?**
   > VisualKit is the only AI support platform with Visual AI - our AI can see your customers' screens in real-time. This means customers can show problems instead of describing them, leading to 46% faster resolution. Plus, we offer a generous free tier and self-hosted options that Intercom and Drift don't.

2. **How does the "AI that sees screens" feature work?**
   > When a customer needs help, they can share their screen with our AI agent. The AI analyzes what's on screen at 1 frame per second (for cost efficiency), understands the context, and provides relevant assistance. All screen data is encrypted and never stored longer than necessary. Customers must explicitly grant permission for screen sharing.

3. **Can I use VisualKit without the screen-sharing feature?**
   > Absolutely. Screen sharing is optional. VisualKit works great as a traditional voice + text AI assistant. The visual capabilities are there when you need them.

**Category: Pricing**

4. **Is the free tier really free forever?**
   > Yes. Our free tier includes 100 AI conversations per month, forever. No credit card required to start. We make money when you grow and need more capacity - not by tricking you with trials that expire.

5. **What counts as a "conversation"?**
   > A conversation is a complete interaction session, regardless of how many messages are exchanged. If a customer chats with your AI for 5 minutes with 20 messages, that's 1 conversation. Conversations are counted when they end (resolved or escalated).

6. **How does VisualKit compare to Intercom's pricing?**
   > Intercom charges $29/seat/month plus $0.99 per AI resolution. For a team of 5 handling 1,000 conversations/month, that's $1,135/month. With VisualKit Pro at $49/month for 1,000 conversations and 5 users, you save over 95%.

**Category: Security & Privacy**

7. **Is screen sharing secure?**
   > Yes. All screen data is transmitted over encrypted WebRTC connections (256-bit encryption). Screen frames are processed in real-time and not stored permanently. Customers must explicitly grant permission, and they can revoke it at any time. We're GDPR compliant and SOC 2 ready.

8. **Can I self-host VisualKit?**
   > Yes, Enterprise customers can deploy VisualKit on their own infrastructure. This gives you complete control over data residency and compliance. Contact our sales team for self-hosted options.

**Category: Integration**

9. **Does VisualKit integrate with my existing tools?**
   > Yes. We integrate with Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, and 20+ other tools via Zapier. Enterprise customers get custom integration support.

10. **Can I use VisualKit with my existing chatbot?**
    > Yes. Our SDK allows you to add Visual AI capabilities to any existing chat interface. You can also use our widget alongside other tools.

## 4.8 Other Pages (Summary)

### Rooms Page (`/rooms`)

**Key elements:**
- List of active/scheduled rooms
- Quick "Create Room" button
- Room cards showing: Name, Status (Active/Scheduled/Ended), Participants, Duration
- Actions: Join, Copy Link, End Room, View Recording

### Schedule Page (`/schedule`)

**Key elements:**
- Calendar view (Week default, Day/Month options)
- Quick scheduling: Click time slot to create
- Integration prompts: "Connect Google Calendar"
- Meeting details: Time, Invitees, Room settings, AI enabled toggle

### Recordings Page (`/recordings`)

**Key elements:**
- List of recordings with thumbnails
- Columns: Date, Room, Duration, Participants, Status (Processing/Ready)
- Actions: Play, Download, Share, Delete
- Premium badge on feature (if applicable)
- Transcript tab alongside video player

### API Keys Page (`/api-keys`)

**Key elements:**
- List of API keys (masked: `vk_live_...abc123`)
- Created date, Last used, Status
- Create key modal with permissions selector
- "Show once" warning on creation
- Usage stats per key

### Team Page (`/team`)

**Key elements:**
- Member list with roles (Admin, Manager, Agent, Viewer)
- Invite modal with email input and role selector
- Pending invites section
- Activity log (optional)

**Sections:**
1. **Account**: Company name, logo, timezone

**Elements:**
- Avatar upload
- Name, email (read-only if SSO)

- Notification preferences

# 5. Visual Design System

## 5.1 Color Palette

### Primary Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | `#3B82F6` | CTAs, links, active states |
| Primary Dark | `#2563EB` | Hover states |
| Primary Light | `#DBEAFE` | Backgrounds, badges |

### Semantic Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Success | `#10B981` | Resolved, positive metrics |
| Warning | `#F59E0B` | Pending, alerts |
| Error | `#EF4444` | Errors, escalated |
| Info | `#6366F1` | Tips, information |

### Neutral Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Gray 900 | `#111827` | Headings |

For dashboard, support dark mode (user preference):
- Background: `#0F172A`

## 5.3 Component Patterns (shadcn/ui)

Use these shadcn/ui components consistently:

| Pattern | Component |
|---------|-----------|
| Primary action | `<Button>` |
| Secondary action | `<Button variant="outline">` |
| Destructive action | `<Button variant="destructive">` |
| Data display | `<Card>` with `<CardHeader>`, `<CardContent>` |
| Data tables | `<Table>` with sortable headers |
| Forms | `<Input>`, `<Select>`, `<Checkbox>` |
| Modals | `<Dialog>` |
| Notifications | `<Toast>` via sonner |
| Navigation | `<NavigationMenu>` or custom sidebar |
| Loading | `<Skeleton>` |

## 5.4 Animation Guidelines

### Micro-interactions

```css
/* Standard transition */
transition: all 150ms ease;

/* Hover lift */
transform: translateY(-2px);
box-shadow: 0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1);

/* Loading pulse */
animation: pulse 2s infinite;
```

### Page Transitions

- Fade in: 200ms ease-out
- Slide in (modals): 250ms ease-out
- Skeleton to content: 300ms fade

### What to Avoid

- No spinners for operations < 300ms
- No animation on every element
- No bouncing or excessive movement
