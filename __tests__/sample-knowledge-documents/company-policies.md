# Company Policies & Guidelines

## Code of Conduct

All employees, contractors, and partners must adhere to our professional standards and ethical guidelines.

### Professional Behavior

- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Communicate professionally in all business contexts
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information
- Report unethical behavior through proper channels

### Workplace Harassment

Zero tolerance policy for:
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Bullying or intimidation
- Retaliation against reporters

**Reporting**: Contact HR at hr@platform.com or use anonymous hotline: 1-800-ETHICS

## Data Privacy & Security

### Customer Data Protection

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Access Control**: Role-based access permissions
- **Data Retention**: Comply with GDPR, CCPA requirements
- **Breach Response**: Notify affected parties within 72 hours

### Employee Responsibilities

1. Use strong, unique passwords for all systems
2. Enable MFA on all accounts
3. Never share credentials
4. Report security incidents immediately
5. Complete annual security training

### Acceptable Use Policy

#### Allowed
- Using company resources for approved business purposes
- Accessing necessary data for job functions
- Reasonable personal use of internet/email

#### Prohibited
- Downloading unauthorized software
- Accessing inappropriate content
- Sharing confidential information externally
- Using company resources for personal business

## Intellectual Property

### Company Ownership

All work product created during employment belongs to the company:
- Source code and documentation
- Designs and inventions
- Business processes and methods
- Customer relationships

### Open Source Usage

**Approved Licenses**:
- MIT, Apache 2.0, BSD
- Allowed for non-core functionality
- Must be documented in dependencies

**Prohibited Licenses**:
- GPL, AGPL (copyleft)
- Commercial licenses
- Restrictive attribution requirements

**Process**:
1. Check license before using any library
2. Document in `DEPENDENCIES.md`
3. Get legal approval for unclear cases

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility

- Employees after 6-month probation period
- Must have suitable home office setup
- Requires manager approval

### Requirements

- Available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time)
- Maintain productivity metrics
- Attend all mandatory meetings
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during work hours

### Equipment

Company provides:
- Laptop and monitor
- Keyboard and mouse
- $500 home office stipend
- Software licenses

Employee responsible for:
- Internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Quiet workspace
- Professional background for video calls

## Time Off & Leave

### Paid Time Off (PTO)

- **Year 1**: 15 days
- **Year 2-3**: 20 days
- **Year 4+**: 25 days
- **Accrual**: 1.25 - 2.08 days per month

**Guidelines**:
- Request PTO at least 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum 10 consecutive days without VP approval
- Cannot carry over more than 5 days

### Holidays

12 company holidays per year:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- President's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas (2 days)
- 2 floating holidays (employee choice)

### Sick Leave

- Unlimited sick days for documented illness
- Doctor's note required for 3+ consecutive days
- Cannot be used for vacation

### Parental Leave

- **Primary Caregiver**: 16 weeks paid
- **Secondary Caregiver**: 6 weeks paid
- Must be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption

## Performance & Development

### Review Cycle

- **Quarterly Check-ins**: Progress on goals
- **Annual Review**: Formal performance evaluation
- **360 Feedback**: Input from peers, manager, direct reports

### Promotion Criteria

**Individual Contributor Track**:
- Junior → Mid: 1-2 years, demonstrated competence
- Mid → Senior: 2-3 years, mentorship, ownership
- Senior → Staff: 3-5 years, architectural impact
- Staff → Principal: 5+ years, company-wide impact

**Management Track**:
- Senior IC → Engineering Manager: Leadership experience
- EM → Senior EM: 2+ years, multiple teams
- Senior EM → Director: 3+ years, department impact
- Director → VP: Strategic leadership

### Learning & Development

Annual budget per employee:
- Junior: $1,000
- Mid: $2,000
- Senior+: $3,000

Approved uses:
- Conferences and workshops
- Online courses and certifications
- Books and learning materials
- Professional memberships

## Compensation & Benefits

### Salary Reviews

- Annual merit increases based on performance
- Market adjustments as needed
- Promotion raises effective immediately

### Benefits Package

**Health Insurance**:
- Medical, dental, vision
- Company pays 80% of premiums
- Covers employee + family

**Retirement**:
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Vests immediately
- Multiple investment options

**Other Benefits**:
- Life insurance (2x salary)
- Disability insurance
- FSA and HSA options
- Commuter benefits
- Gym membership ($50/month reimbursement)

### Equity Compensation

**Stock Options**:
- Granted at hire and promotions
- 4-year vesting schedule (1-year cliff)
- 10-year expiration
- Exercise within 90 days of termination

**Refreshers**:
- Annual grants for strong performers
- Retention grants at discretion

## Expense Reimbursement

### Approved Expenses

**Travel**:
- Airfare (economy class)
- Hotels ($200/night max in major cities)
- Meals ($75/day per diem)
- Ground transportation (Uber, public transit)

**Technology**:
- Monitor, keyboard, mouse
- Headphones
- Software subscriptions (with approval)
- Phone plan ($75/month max)

**Professional Development**:
- Conference tickets and travel
- Courses and certifications
- Books and materials
- Professional memberships

### Process

1. Submit expense report within 30 days
2. Include receipts for expenses >$25
3. Manager approval required
4. Reimbursement within 2 pay periods

## Termination & Offboarding

### Resignation

- Provide 2 weeks notice (4 weeks for senior roles)
- Complete knowledge transfer
- Return all company property
- Exit interview with HR

### Company-Initiated Termination

**For Cause**:
- Immediate termination
- No severance
- Examples: Theft, harassment, violence

**Performance-Based**:
- Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) first
- 30-90 day improvement period
- Severance if terminated after PIP

**Layoff/Restructuring**:
- 2 weeks notice or pay in lieu
- Severance: 1 week per year of service (min 4 weeks)
- COBRA health insurance extension
- Outplacement services

## Whistleblower Protection

Employees who report violations in good faith are protected from retaliation:

- Report to: ethics@platform.com
- Anonymous hotline: 1-800-ETHICS
- No adverse employment actions
- Confidentiality maintained
